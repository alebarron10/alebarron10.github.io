Overview

Cookie Clicker Pro is an idle game where you click on a giant cookie to earn cookies and gradually build an empire. As you progress, you can buy automated buildings like cursors, grandmas, and factories to increase your cookie production. The goal is to continually expand and upgrade your empire, making your cookie-making power unstoppable.

How To Play

  1. Click the Giant Cookie: Start by clicking on the giant cookie to earn cookies, and track your progress at the top of the screen.
  2. Buy Automating Buildings: Once you have enough cookies, purchase cursors and grandmas to automate your cookie production.Check how many hearts you have (5 to start), and click on a revealed enemy with a power level equal to or less than that.
  3. Unlock More Structures: As you progress, unlock powerful buildings like factories, farms, and cookie mines to increase your output.
  4. Upgrade Your Empire: Use your cookies to buy upgrades that enhance your baking power.
  5. Expand and Dominate: Keep building and upgrading to grow your cookie empire and become an unstoppable cookie-making machine.
