Celebrate Your Ninja at Rogue Play!

Looking for a fun and unforgettable way to celebrate in Northern Colorado? Rogue Play Trampoline & Ninja Center has the perfect setup for birthdays, sports banquets, reunions, and more. We offer high-energy activities and cozy party spaces to suit every occasion!

About Our Parties

  • Each party lasts 2 hours and includes access to our trampoline and ninja zones
  • Choose between Rogue Play #1 or #2 areas — both are located on the main floor and offer the same experience
  • Toddler party packages available for guests of honor 6 years or under (upstairs area; no elevator available)
  • Older guests still have full access to all attractions
  • Parkour Party Area: Private space for up to 50 guests — great for larger groups
  • No age limit — everyone is welcome to celebrate!
  • Spectators do not count toward your party participant total
  • Want exclusive access? Ask about our Private Party Option

Party Rules & Policies

  • A signed liability waiver is required for all participants at check-in (available on our homepage)
  • Deposits are non-refundable — no exceptions
  • Walk-in parties in common or waiting areas are not permitted
  • Grippy trampoline socks are required for all guests — bring your own to save!
  • We've lowered party package prices by eliminating extras like socks
